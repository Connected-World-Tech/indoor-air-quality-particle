Mold

Temperature
The recommended temperatures are related to mold growth, not personal comfort. If you find that household members have symptoms as the temperature rises - lowering the temperature can help to reduce symptoms.

Reducing temperatures (and adding more clothes when needed) can help when there is a mold problem. The VOC's emitted by mold particles will offgas less at lower temperatures.

Symptoms
Increase in the following symptoms :
  • Eye, nose and throat irritation
  • Headaches
  • Dizziness
  • Nausea/Vomiting
  • Worsening of Asthma symptoms
